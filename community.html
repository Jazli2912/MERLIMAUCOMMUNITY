    • FINANCIAL FACILITIES

    There are numerous Financial Facilities for the citizens of Merlimau regarding their financial inquiries.


    The Financial Facilities offered at Merlimau are:

  • Bank Simpanan Nasional Giro, Jalan Jasin

  • Tabung Haji, Bangunan Mara
  • ATM Bank Islam at Politeknik Merlimau
  • ATM Public Bank in 7-Eleven Taman Muhibbah, Merlimau Utara
  • Agrobank, Bandar Baru Merlimau Utara

    Multiple Public Services buildings have been constructed to aid the citizens of Merlimau if there are any emergency to occur.

  • Government Clinic, Jalan Jasin,
  • Private Clinic and Pharmaceutical
  • Bus Stops and Pos Office
  • Police Station around 1 kilometer south, near Muar.
